Why Choose Bright Stars?

Our team is passionate, experienced, and committed to making a difference by addressing a critical issue: the high unemployment rate in South Africa, particularly among youth. Youth unemployment remains one of the country's biggest challenges, exacerbated by a lack of skills in many industries. These challenges are largely a result of poor education standards and the low quality of results achieved by learners. Careers in Maths and Science are in high demand, yet many students shy away from these subjects due to perceived difficulty. This issue is even more pronounced in communities near mining operations, where local industries are forced to import highly skilled individuals because many learners fail to meet the minimum requirements for developing a skilled workforce within their own communities.

Holistic Approach

We take a holistic approach to tutoring, believing that all students can learn and succeed, though each in their own way and at their own pace.

Proven Track Record

Since its founding in 2017, Bright Stars has experienced steady growth and become a household name for academic interventions in Gauteng. The company has expanded over the years and now boasts a team of three directors, five permanent employees, and more than 30 part-time staff. It currently supports 33 schools and serves over 2,000 learners through its programs, with a strong commitment to growth and maintaining high-quality services.

Community-Centric

Our academic intervention services are tailored to meet the specific needs of the communities we serve, with a focus on youth education. We prioritize hiring from within these communities, ensuring local engagement and empowerment. We are committed to long-term development and fostering sustainable growth.

Get Involved

WE are working towards our vision through the design and formulation of quality tutoring lessons that enable learners to unlock their potential and excel in their academics allowing them to access careers and universities of their choice.
